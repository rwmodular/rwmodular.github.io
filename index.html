nQuencer

nQuencer is a eurorack module that turns the 16n faderbank into a step sequencer.

  • 16 step, 2x8 step, 8 step transposed by 8 step & dj mode (toggle between two 8 steps)
  • built in quantizer
  • sequence direction, probability & more
